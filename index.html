What is Chronos?

We, Chronos, are honoured to be the first debating association in Eindhoven, the Netherlands. As an international community, we seek to stimulate thought, verbal skills and presentational skills in English. Our core values are centralized around excellence, professionalism and inclusiveness.

Are you looking to improve your communication skills, build your CV or simply to enjoy and experience debating? Come join us for debates on Mondays from 18:00-20:00 in Audi 10 at the TU/e.

Current Board

Michael Stolk

President

As chairman of Chronos, Michael is responsible for long-term goals, the position of Chronos in Eindhoven and motivating the rest of the board to contribute their unique set of interdisciplinary perspectives. Michael’s interest in the scientific method is a common denominator of his arguments.

Noël Coenraad

Vice-President

Our vice-chairman Noel is responsible for the formal aspects of the association, including legal matters, and setting up important processes in the founding year of Chronos to ensure long-term sustainability. His strong understanding of other cultures gives Noel an edge in debating.

Wayan Traversat

Treasurer

As treasurer, Wayan, is responsible for keeping tabs on the financial matters within Chronos, using his predisposition to logic to come up with innovative takes on matters. It is this same logic that makes Wayan’s argumentation so easy to accept.

Avinash Nayak

Internal Affairs

Avinash takes care of internal affairs within Chronos, including organizing events and making sure our most important activity, debating, is kept in high regard. Avinash is also responsible for our member base. In debates, he uses his strong analytical skills to convince the judges.

Geert Vergoossen

External Affairs

Geert is responsible for the business development of Chronos, focusing primarily on sponsoring and serving as a liaison to sister associations in the Netherlands and internationally. Geert’s natural social skills give him a strong confidence in conveying his thoughts.

Hugo Bezombes

Public Relations

Hugo is responsible for communication and public relations. He takes advantage of his fundamental understanding of international relations to mark off his territory of political debating. Hugo’s interests in linguistics make him exceptional in conveying his message.
